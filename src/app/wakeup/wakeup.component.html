Wake Up

You wake up. You don't want to. Everything aches, from your feet to your head. Even the sun pounding against the blinds in your livingroom hurts, like you can hear the photons knocking to get in. You drank too much last night.

At least you made it home from the party. Or was the party here? It's all a blur.

Your eyes swim back to focus and your gaze lands on the clock. 12:00. Noon. Something about that time nags at you, pulsing along with the throb in your skull. You'll have to do something about that.
